Explore Norwich

Discover some of the finest attractions Norwich has to offer.

Norwich Cathedral

Walk through centuries of history beneath Norwich Cathedral’s towering arches and intricate stonework. Marvel at the stunning stained glass windows, each telling its own story, and wander the peaceful cloisters once walked by monks. Find out more

Norwich Market

Discover the UK’s largest outdoor market, offering fresh produce and global cuisines. From Thai curries to Spanish tapas, this market is a vibrant culinary hub filled with rich history and culture. Find out more

Norfolk Broads

A peaceful escape into nature, the Norfolk Broads offer breathtaking views, calm waters, and picturesque villages. Perfect for boating, birdwatching, and watching the sun rise over golden waters. Find out more

Mousehold Heath

The best panoramic view of Norwich, Mousehold Heath overlooks the city’s landmarks and offers a peaceful nature retreat. Whether at sunrise or sunset, it’s a perfect spot to take in the skyline. Find out more
