Restaurant & Bar

Fine food. Signature drinks. Timeless ambiance.

The Earl’s Dining Room

Step into timeless elegance at The Earl’s Dining Room, where classic sophistication meets modern luxury. With its ornate décor, soft ambient lighting, and rich wood accents, this exclusive dining space is perfect for intimate gatherings, private dinners, or executive events. Whether hosting a celebratory meal or a business function, guests will enjoy impeccable service, exquisite cuisine, and a refined atmosphere worthy of nobility.

The Sandringham Suite

Experience grandeur in The Sandringham Suite, a luxurious function room inspired by the opulence of Norfolk’s royal heritage. With its high ceilings, plush furnishings, and refined architectural details, the suite offers a majestic setting for weddings, corporate events, and gala dinners. Its spacious layout, combined with state-of-the-art facilities, ensures an unforgettable event in a space where tradition and luxury harmoniously blend.

The Norfolk Lounge & Bar

Unwind in the sophisticated Norfolk Lounge & Bar, where chic design meets a relaxed, inviting atmosphere. Whether you're enjoying a hand-crafted cocktail, sipping fine wine, or indulging in expertly prepared small plates, the space offers the perfect setting for both casual dining and lively evening gatherings. With its contemporary décor, ambient lighting, and welcoming vibe, it’s the ideal spot to socialise, celebrate, or simply relax after a long day.

The Parlour Tea Room

Step into the charming elegance of The Parlour Tea Room, where the tradition of afternoon tea is elevated in an intimate, cozy setting. Surrounded by classic décor and soft lighting, guests are invited to enjoy a delightful selection of finger sandwiches, freshly baked scones with clotted cream, and a variety of pastries, all paired with premium loose-leaf teas. Perfect for both intimate gatherings and special celebrations, this inviting space offers a refined yet relaxed atmosphere to enjoy the finest of British tea traditions.

Bites & Cocktails

Indulge in the perfect pairing of gourmet bites and signature cocktails, expertly crafted to elevate your experience. Served in The Norfolk Lounge & Bar, each cocktail is a work of art, complemented by an array of delicious small plates designed to tantalise your taste buds. From innovative, refreshing cocktails to savory bites made with the finest ingredients, every moment spent here promises an unforgettable culinary journey in a stylish, relaxed atmosphere.
